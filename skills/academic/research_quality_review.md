# Skill: Research Quality Review

**Type:** Domain — Academic
**Applicable agents:** paper_review_agent, ai_trend_radar_agent

## Purpose
Assesses whether a research source is credible, relevant, and usable for applied decision-making.

## Required Checks
- Research question and contribution are explicit.
- Evidence type is clear: empirical, theoretical, benchmark, survey, or position paper.
- Dataset, sample, benchmark, or corpus is named when applicable.
- Limitations are separated from interpretation.
- Claims are not stronger than the evidence supports.

## Output
Use this structure:
- **Quality verdict:** usable / usable with caveats / not decision-grade
- **Evidence strength:** high / medium / low
- **Main limitation:** one sentence
- **Operational relevance:** one sentence
