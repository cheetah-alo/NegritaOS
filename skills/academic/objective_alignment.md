# Skill: Objective Alignment

**Type:** Domain — Academic
**Applicable agents:** tfm_evaluator_agent, proposal_validator_agent

## Purpose
Checks that title, problem, objectives, methodology, and expected outputs answer the same research question.

## Checks
- The title names the object of study.
- The problem statement explains the gap or need.
- The general objective answers the problem directly.
- Specific objectives are measurable and non-overlapping.
- The methodology is a means, not the objective itself.

## Output
Report aligned items, misaligned items, and the minimum rewrite needed to restore coherence.
