# Skill: Methodology Deconstruction

**Type:** Domain — Academic
**Applicable agents:** paper_review_agent

## Purpose
Breaks a paper's method into assumptions, data, procedure, evaluation, and limits.

## Structure
- **Method summary:** what was done, in one paragraph.
- **Inputs:** datasets, variables, prompts, benchmarks, or source material.
- **Procedure:** steps that could be reproduced.
- **Evaluation:** metrics, baselines, or qualitative criteria.
- **Threats to validity:** internal, external, construct, or operational risks.

## Rule
If a method cannot be reproduced from the available paper, state the missing information explicitly.
